Who We Are

Rooted in Community.
Driven by Change.

Founded in October 2025, CEAN is a registered Kenyan community-based organisation operating under the NGO Co-ordination Act, 1990 — committed to building thriving, equitable communities across Kenya.

2025Founded in Kenya
Our Vision & Mission

A Kenya where every citizen thrives equally

Vision: A thriving Kenyan community where empowered citizens enjoy equal opportunities, economic self-reliance, environmental sustainability, and the protection of gender rights and reproductive health.

Mission: To promote community development through economic empowerment, environmental conservation, and improved access to reproductive health — while firmly addressing gender-based violence.

What We Do

Our Programmes

Eight interconnected thematic areas where CEAN works alongside communities to create lasting, measurable change across Kenya.

🌿

Environmental Conservation & Sustainability

Tree-planting initiatives, climate action campaigns, green clubs in schools, eco-friendly agriculture, and clean energy education to protect Kenya's natural resources for future generations.

💼

Economic Empowerment & Livelihoods

Micro-enterprise development, Village Savings and Loan Associations (VSLAs), financial literacy training, and market access support for women, youth, and local entrepreneurs.

⚖️

Gender Equality & GBV Prevention

Counselling, legal support, and awareness campaigns to prevent gender-based violence. Advocacy for equal representation and access to resources for women and marginalised groups.

🏥

Health & Reproductive Rights

Medical outreach camps, reproductive health forums, mental health talks, sanitary towel distribution, immunisation drives, and mobile clinic partnerships across communities.

📚

Education & Skills Development

Bursaries, learning materials, mentorship, ICT training, learning centres, and vocational skills for children and youth from disadvantaged backgrounds across Kenya.

💧

Water, Sanitation & Hygiene (WASH)

Water harvesting systems, community sanitation drives, hygiene education, menstrual health programmes, and sustainable water use in rural and peri-urban Kenya.

🕊️

Peace, Leadership & Civic Education

Conflict resolution workshops, youth ambassador training, inter-community dialogue forums, and civic education that builds responsible, engaged Kenyan citizens.

🎨

Culture, Arts & Talent Development

Sports events, arts competitions, cultural heritage documentation, and youth innovation hubs that celebrate Kenyan talent and nurture the next generation of creative leaders.

Stories of Change

Impact in Action

Every programme we run creates real stories of transformation — here is a glimpse of the change CEAN is building across Kenyan communities.

🌿 Environment

Green Clubs Take Root in Schools

CEAN launched environmental stewardship clubs in community schools, empowering young Kenyans to become frontline environmental advocates — planting trees and leading clean-up campaigns in their neighbourhoods.

💼 Economic Empowerment

Women-Led VSLAs Changing Livelihoods

Through Village Savings and Loan Associations, CEAN has helped women in underserved communities access micro-capital, launch small businesses, and build household financial resilience.

"
CEAN gave our community a voice and the tools to use it. The training, the support, the advocacy — it changed the way we see our own potential.
— Community Member, CEAN Programme Participant, Kenya
🏥 Health Outreach

Mobile Clinics Reaching the Unreached

CEAN's health outreach camps brought screenings, reproductive health education, and basic medical care to communities far from formal health facilities — bridging Kenya's healthcare access gap.

Funding Partners

Why Fund CEAN?

CEAN is a credible, transparent, and accountable partner for institutional funders, grant-making bodies, and impact investors committed to community-led development in Kenya.

🏛️

Formal Legal Registration

Registered under Kenya's NGO Co-ordination Act, 1990. Fully compliant with the Constitution of Kenya (2010) and all applicable national laws governing community-based organisations.

📊

Transparent Governance

Democratically elected Executive Committee with defined roles, two-year term limits, and decision-making through a General Assembly. Full constitutional framework and member accountability in place.

🔍

External Annual Audit

CEAN engages an independent external auditor annually. Audit reports are presented to the Executive Committee and shared with all members at the Annual General Meeting (AGM).

📅

Quarterly Financial Reporting

The Treasurer presents quarterly financial statements to the Executive Committee. All financial records are maintained for a minimum of seven years for full accountability and compliance.

🌍

SDG & Vision 2030 Alignment

All programmes are designed in alignment with the SDGs (1, 3, 5, 10, 13, 15) and Kenya Vision 2030's social, economic, and environmental development pillars.
